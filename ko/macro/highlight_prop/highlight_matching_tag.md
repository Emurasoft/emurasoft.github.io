# HighlightMatchingTag 속성

Corresponds to the **Highlight**
**Matching Tag** 체크 박스 in the [**Highlight (2)** 탭](../../dlg/properties/highlight2/index) of 구성 속성.

#### \[JavaScript\]

_b_ =
object. **HighlightMatchingTag**;

object. **HighlightMatchingTag** = _b_;

#### \[VBScript\]

_b_ =
object. **HighlightMatchingTag**

object. **HighlightMatchingTag** = _b_

## 버전

Supported on 엠에디터 Professional Version 13.0.4 또는 later.
